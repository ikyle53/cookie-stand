Salmon Cookies

Fresh Ingredients

Our ingrediants are made from organic farms in our local area from using free trade methods. The wheat that supplies our flour is sourced, ground, processed, and packages all in the northwest. All of our fish ingredients are caught from the wild by local fishers. Everything is sourced from locals and not a big corporation.

Family owned

Pat has been in the business for over 23 years. She's been baking all her life and helped consult over 100 businesses to get on their feet running. She's now the proud owner of Salmon cookies which is ran by her own family of children and grandchildren. Drop by any time and become part of the Family yourself!

Delicious and nutritious

Our cookies have over 1 dozen flavors! Each of our flavors are naturally flavored and not filled with preservatives you can't pronounce. We add vitamins and minerals that give your body proper nutrients and we also serve cookies for most dietary needs like diabetes, weight loss, and much more. Contact us today and we'll be sure to meet your needs!

5 Location Worldwide

We've gone international and you can hit us up in one of our 5 locations spread across the globe. Each store has its own theme, merch, and flavors. All ingredients for each store is provided at the local level and according to its food safety laws. Our bakers are world class trained in some of the most stringent cooking environments to go above and beyond food safety rules.
